Welcome to the 2024 Zoo Census. All responses to this form will be anonymous. Please answer as many questions as you feel comfortable, though no questions are required. If a question is not applicable to you or you would prefer not to answer, please leave it blank.

Consent

By submitting this form, you consent to your responses being used exclusively for aggregate data analysis. This means that your individual answers will not be shared or published in any way that identifies you personally. The information collected will be combined with other responses to understand broader community trends and insights.

If you have any questions or concerns about how your data will be used, please feel free to contact me at aster@zoolandia.org.
