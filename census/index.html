2024 Zoo Census

Welcome to the 2024 Zoo Census. All responses to this form will be anonymous. Please answer as many questions as you feel comfortable, though no questions are required. If a question is not applicable to you or you would prefer not to answer, please leave it blank. Please feel free to use the 'other' option to give as much detail as you like in your responses.

Consent

By submitting this form, you consent to your responses being used exclusively for aggregate data analysis. This means that your individual answers will not be shared or published in any way that identifies you personally. The information collected will be combined with other responses to understand broader community trends and insights. Information specific to one individual will be omitted from data analyses to avoid identification. The results of this census will be shared publicly on my website, animalboysister.zoolandia.org, and the Zooey Dot Pub magazine.

If you have any questions or concerns about how your data will be used, please feel free to contact me at aster@zoolandia.org.
