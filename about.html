biromantic - I'm attracted to both sexes for both humans and non-human animals, as well as genderqueer and intersex people.

zoosexual - I'm attracted to non-human animals. my primary attraction is large dog breeds, though I'm also attracted to other larger animals such as horses, cattle, sheep, deer, large cats, bears, crocodillians, etc.
