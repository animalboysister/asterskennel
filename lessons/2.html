lesson 2 - complex sentences!

vocabulary!

  • cv - object marker (obj)
  • vcvvc - can, able, possible, ability
  • vvccv - know, understand, information
  • vvcvc - see, look, appear, seem
  • vvcvv - do, work, activity, action
  • ccvccv - have, own, possess
  • ccvcvc - think, mind, ponder, reason
  • ccvcvc - feel, experience, emotion
  • ccvvcc - happen, occur
  • ccvcvv - hear, listen, obey
  • cvccvc - live, life, living
  • cvccvv - die, death, dead
  • cvcvcc - true, real, exist

the object of a sentence is the one who the verb is done to. in the sentence 'alice loves rover', 'rover' is the object. in both english and zoophona, the object is placed after the verb. in zoophona, the object is seperated from the verb by placing the object marker cv between them.

arf a wrarfa wa woof vcc v ccvccv cv cvvc 1p sbj have obj animal I have an animal

a sentence can have any number of subjects or objects, with each seperated by their respective markers.

ufa a arf a oofuf wa oof vcv v vcc v vvcvc cv vvc 2p sbj 1p sbj see obj 3p you and I see them

more complex sentences can be created by chaining together smaller phrases with a comma.

ufa a wrufuf wa wruf, woof a arfuf vcv v ccvcvc cv ccvc, cvvc v vccvc 1p sbj think obj this, animal sbj good I think that animals are good
