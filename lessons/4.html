lesson 4 - auxiliary verbs!

vocabulary!

  • vcvcvc - near, side, close, nearby
  • vcvcvv - far, away, remove, distance
  • vcvvcc - side
  • vcvvcv - touch, contact
  • vvccvc - inside, centre, internal
  • vvccvv - word, name
  • vvcvcc - body, state, shape, form
  • vvcvcv - kind, variety, sort, type
  • vvcvvc - part, piece, section
  • ccvccvc - house, building, home
  • ccvccvv - come, become, arise
  • ccvcvcc - what, which
  • ccvcvcv = begin, start, origin

an auxiliary verb is a verb that adds extra meaning to the main verb of a sentence. in the sentence 'I love to kiss dogs', 'love' is the auxiliary verb modifying the main verb 'to kiss'. in both english and zoophona, auxiliary verbs are placed before the main verb.
